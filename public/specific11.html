SPECIFICATIONS:-

OPPO Reno 2 comes with a Kryo 470 octa-core processor (a 2.2GHz dual-core and a 1.8GHz Hexa Core) setup.

It is coupled with a massive 8GB RAM and an Adreno 618 GPU that handles the graphical performance of the device.

The whole operations are done by Qualcomm Snapdragon 730G chipset.

Model: Reno 2series

SIM Slot(s): Dual SIM, GSM+GSM
